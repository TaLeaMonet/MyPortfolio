About Me


Greetings! My name is TaLea Carpenter and I'm a Software Engineer apprentice looking to creatively blend my passion for music and technology!
I'm currently an apprentice at a program called Techtonica, which focuses on training minority and low-income women and preparing them to enter into tech. The stack we're covering is the MERN Stack.


The Journey

It all started with a trip to the grocery store one day back in 2014. While heading for the checkout, I noticed a man wearing a t-shirt that read: "Coding Is The New Literacy". It sparked my interest because as someone who likes to think of themselves as literate to some degree, I value reading and learning new things. Although it sparked my interest, it wasn't something I thought I could learn.
